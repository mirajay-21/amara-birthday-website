Love,Birthdays.

Birthdays,
The days I once waited for with wild anticipation,
The days I laid out special clothes the night before,
The days I wished would come twice a year-
Ah, the beauty of childish glee and innocent wonder.

Birthdays,
The days I quietly dread now,
The days that whisper of time slipping by,
The days I wish would slow their pace-
Just enough to let me feel like I'm not being left behind.

Birthdays,
The 21st of May-
A date forever etched in my heart,
A date I'll always look forward to,
A date wrapped in beauty, warmth and meaning,
A date I love.

No matter what has happened,
Is happening,
Or will happen-
Birthdays- my birthdays-
I'll always cherish.

-Amarachi Onyele.
