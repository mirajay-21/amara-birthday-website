So anyone who knows me knows that I'm a sucker for memories in the form of pictures and videos. Cheesy? Yeah, I know.

But will that make me change? ABSOLUTELY NOT!

Because after all that's said and done, we need to be reminded exactly how we felt at a particular time.

The joy, sadness, laughter, feelings, people, jokes, banter, music and fights are more vivid when we can see them again in that particular moment - like a trip back in time.

Because, trust me, we'll need to - or feel like - experiencing them again.

Maybe because we miss who we were at that particular moment or just to look back and see how far we've come.

So, take that picture. Record that video. Who knows when you'd need it.
