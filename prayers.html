My Birthday Prayers.

  • May I find my direction this new year. May I find clarity and purpose in every area of my life.
  • May my family have long life, peace of mind and the strength to enjoy all the goodness that life has to offer.
  • May my love be covered in understanding, patience, laughter and may love never feel like a chore.
  • May my friends always feel seen, supported and loved. May we show up for one another in ways that truly matter.
  • May my finances flourish. May I never lack what I need and may I always have more than enough to bless others too.
  • May the right doors open before me - the kind of opportunities that align with who I am and where I want to go.
  • May my mind and body remain clear. May I never take the little things for granted.
  • May this birthday bring all the answers to my unanswered prayers and may I also grow in my spiritual life with Christ.

AMEN
